PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated: February 2024

1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to LOGICZ – Block Puzzle ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game.

By using LOGICZ, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Anonymous Users

When you first launch the game, a unique anonymous ID is automatically created via Firebase Authentication. We collect:

  • Anonymous user ID (auto-generated)
  • Game scores (for leaderboard)
  • Game statistics stored locally on your device

Registered Users

If you choose to save your progress with an account, we additionally collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name / username
  • Game statistics (high score, games played, achievements)
  • Challenge history

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To display your score on global leaderboards
  • To save and restore your game progress across devices
  • To track your achievements and statistics
  • To improve the game experience

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. FIREBASE & GOOGLE SERVICES

LOGICZ uses the following Firebase services provided by Google LLC:

  • Firebase Authentication – User identity management
  • Cloud Firestore – Score and profile storage

These services may collect usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

5. DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

Your data is stored securely using Firebase infrastructure with encryption in transit and at rest. Access is restricted to authenticated users for their own data.

Local game data (settings, board state) is stored on your device using secure local storage.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Anonymous user data may be retained for up to 90 days after last activity.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data

To delete your account and all associated data, visit our Data Deletion Page or use the "Delete Account" option in the game's Settings screen.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

LOGICZ is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10. CONTACT US

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: aykuterd@gmail.com
