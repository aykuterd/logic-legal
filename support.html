FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Drag blocks from the tray onto the 9×9 board. Fill a complete row, column, or 3×3 block to clear it and earn points. The game ends when no more pieces can be placed.

Logicz offers four modes: Classic (endless, high score), Zen (no game over, relaxed), Time Attack (3-minute countdown with combo time bonuses), and Target Score (reach a goal in Easy / Medium / Hard / Expert difficulty).

A new puzzle is available every day. Complete it to earn up to 3 stars. Play 7 days in a row to become Weekly Champion, or 30 days in a row for Monthly Legend status.

Themes and badges are unlocked by reaching score milestones, completing combos and streaks, finishing daily challenges, and playing specific game modes. Check the Achievements screen in the app to track your progress.

The global leaderboard ranks players by their best Target Score results. Sign in to save your progress and appear on the board. Rankings update in real time.
